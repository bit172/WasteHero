About Us

Waste Hero is a non-profit company that aims to reduce food leftovers by Canadian city.
It offers the opportunity to consumer to purchase food of fair quality that will get wasted
for reasons such as canceled order, surplus of food, or that the meal is not fresh anymore to
be served by a restaurants for a discounted price.
